Objective: Survey on the Range of Awareness of Internet Usage in Village

Detailed Report

Day 1: Introduction

Our team initiated the internet fraud awareness campaign in the village by engaging with local leaders and community members. We discussed the prevalence of internet fraud and its impact on individuals and families. The importance of staying vigilant and informed was emphasized, setting the tone for the days ahead.

Day 2: Educational Sessions

We conducted educational sessions to equip villagers with knowledge to identify and prevent internet fraud. Common tactics such as phishing and identity theft were explained, and practical safety tips were provided. Villagers actively participated, eager to learn and safeguard themselves.

Day 3: Interactive Workshops

Building on previous lessons, interactive workshops helped villagers simulate real-life fraud scenarios. These exercises improved their ability to recognize suspicious activities and respond effectively, empowering them to stay alert online.

Day 4: Digital Literacy Training

We dedicated the day to training villagers on essential digital skills. Topics included creating strong passwords, safe online transactions, and ethical internet usage. The training enhanced both confidence and digital responsibility among participants.

Day 5: Awareness Walk and Outreach

An awareness walk was organized through the village, engaging residents and distributing informational materials. Villagers enthusiastically participated and extended outreach to nearby communities, promoting online safety across a broader audience.

Day 6: Reflection and Future Planning

The final day involved reflecting on our achievements and planning sustainable awareness strategies. Villagers expressed gratitude and discussed maintaining cybersecurity vigilance through collaboration with local authorities.
